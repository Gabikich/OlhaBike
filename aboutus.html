Who are we

At OlhaBike, we believe that the right bike fit is the key to unlocking your cycling potential. Whether you're aiming for peak performance or seeking comfort on long rides, our expert team is here to provide personalized bike fitting solutions tailored to your unique needs.

Our history

OlhaBike started with a deep passion for downhill biking. Driven by our love for the sport, we decided to open our own bike workshop, offering top-quality service—the best in the region. Since then, we have continued to grow professionally, combining our passion for biking with expertise to provide the best and safest service for every rider.

Why Choose Us?

  • Expertise: Our certified fitters have years of experience helping cyclists of all levels achieve their perfect fit.
  • Personalized Service: We take the time to understand your goals, body mechanics, and riding style
    to ensure a fit that's perfect for you.
  • Technology: We use the latest tools and techniques to analyse your fit and
    optimize your riding experience.
